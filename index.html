The cause of injury in accidents also varies significantly depending on the overall severity of the accident. 'Transportation Incidents' accounted for well over a third of deaths at work in 2016, but were much less often the cause of a non-fatal injury. Conversely, 'Overexertion and Bodily Reaction' led the field for injuries in the workplace, but in no cases led to a death at work.
